Robotics For Kids

What Is Robotics For Kids?


Robotics for kids is an upcoming field of study that many parents want their kids to learn. In online robotics classes for kids learners apply principles of maths, science, coding & mechanic to create real robots. As this is a multi-disciplinary field,taking robotics classes helps learners get a basic understanding of coding and enhances their mathematical skills as well.

Benefits of Robotics For Kids


Robotics is a multidisciplinary field involving the study and application of math, coding, electronics and mechanics. Robotics is useful for Kids as kids who take up robotics, therefore,develop a wide knowledge and skills particularly critical thinking and creative skills. Starting early with robotics is also known to help develop motor skills in kids.

Right age to start Robotics for kids


Kids can start learning robotics from age 5 onwards. At age 5 kids can be introduced to building blocks of robotics such as the input sensors, microcontrollers and output devices. From age 7 onwards, kids can be introduced to Basic circuit design and simulation using a simulation software like Tinkercad. Further on form age 9-10 onwards kids can be introduced to designing and programming physical robotics using a micro controller such as a BBC micro:bit or arduino uno.

Future of robotics for kids


Robotics and robots have a wide range of applications. Robots are no longer limited to industrial usages such as in manufacturing or warehouses but are rapidly becoming a part of the household. Case in point is automated home robots for home cleaning, grill cleaning, pool cleaning and lawn moving.Robots are also available as pets for kids. Sensors which are the basic building blocks of robots are used extensively in automobiles, locking systems, and household electronics.
As per world economic forum, future of jobs report 2020 AI and machine learning specialists and process automation specialists are among top 5 jobs worldwide with increasing demand. Learning robotics for kids therefore opens up doors to a lot of future job opportunities in the field of industrial automation, warehousing, defence, automobiles and home automation.

Robotics And Maths

Does robotics require a lot of math?


This is one of the most common queries parents ask when signing up for our robotics classes for kids. Robotics is a multi-disciplinary field involving application of all STEM blocks (Science, Technology, Engineer, Mathematics). In a typical robotics course, kids will learn about the different building blocks of a robot – sensors, micro-controllers and output devices.

Kids will understand and apply electronics, math and coding concepts to program the robot and use mechanics to assemble the robot. So a basic knowledge of math is always helpful in beginner robotics. Heavy math is required only if you are taking advanced robotics and designing complex robots for scientific or industrial purposes.

Difference between robot and robotics

A robot is a programmable device that can complete a specified task or task. A typical robot consists of sensors such as light and sound sensors, a microcontroller and output devices such as motors. Robots can be designed with different levels of autonomy from fully controlled to semi-autonomous to fully autonomous.By extension Robotics is the field of study of design and application of Robots.
